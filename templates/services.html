Tailor's Thimble Bespoke Process

The process for each garment usually takes between 3-4 weeks from the first consultation to the last fitting. Prices vary depending on the complexity of the design and the fabrics chosen.

Joe will need to meet you in person at least 5 times throughout the stages of the process. Below is a list of the stages and meetings required to complete your Bespoke garment.

We would urge you to book your consultation at least 6 months in advance to avoid disappointment!

Consultation Details & Design Fabrics First Fitting Second Fitting Pick Up

A no-strings-attached meeting, where you can chat with the Master Tailor about the requirements for your Bespoke garment. Joe will talk you through the process and give you an approximation of dates and price for your finished garment.

Bring your ideas and Joe will start the unique design process with you. He will get to know you and your needs and take your measurements.

You will meet with Joe and together visit the textile store in search for the fabrics he will be cutting for your garment.

Joe will have cut your fabric and started working on it at this stage. He will have a basic shape on your garment which you will get to try out. You will get to see it taking form as he works on it.

At this stage the garment will have taken shape and Joe will get you to try it on and move around in order to make sure it fits you well and you feel comfortable in it. His keen eye will spot any quick alterations that need to be made.

This is the stage where you get to try on the finished bespoke garment. Joe will have worked on the details before this final appointment and will make sure you are 100% happy with the finished product. Any small alterations will be done before you take it home.
